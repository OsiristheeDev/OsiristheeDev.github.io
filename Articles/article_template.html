Artist of the Year: Tyla

South Africa’s rising star takes global pop by storm.

The first time I saw Tyla perform, I knew the world would catch on soon. With her hypnotic sound and unmistakable stage presence, she embodies that magnetic energy all the greats do. South Africa, you’ve got a comet on your hands.

Style & Presence

Her fashion sense? Immaculate. From slick latex to soft mesh, Tyla knows how to use style to underscore her story.

“I’m not trying to be the next anyone — I’m trying to be the first Tyla.”

Whether onstage or off, she’s rewriting what it means to be a global African pop star — and we’re lucky to witness it.
