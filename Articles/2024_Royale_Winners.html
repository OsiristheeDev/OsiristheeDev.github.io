2023 Royale Award Winners

Eligibility Period: Jan 1st, 2023 - Dec 20th, 2023

Artist of The Year: Beyoncé

New Artist of The Year

Any artist or collective who released an album this year that inspired me to explore their discography for the first time.

New Artist Of The Year

That Mexican OT

From Paul Wall to Megan Thee Stallion, Texas rap has been on. That Mexican OT is its latest herald. His latest album: Lonestar Luchador features a unique combination of regional Tejano music with Southern trap on songs like Barrio to making straight-up saloon music with a modern twist on songs like Cowboy Killer. With a flow that is as smooth as it is interchangeable That Mexican OT embodies Texas rap and pushes it into a bold new direction.

Collab of The Year

Dalie - Kamo Mphela feat Tyler ICU, Khalil Harrison, Baby S.O.N

Dalie is a group project where everyone did their part, from Kamo Mphela, the hook's headliner and star of the video, to Tyler ICU, one of the producers. Tyler ICU is a certified hitmaker who has produced some of the most infectious Amapiano records of the year. A perfect example of sonic synthesis.

Live Performance of The Year

Renaissance World Tour, Louisville - Beyoncé

16 years of watching her through a screen still didn't prepare me for the live experience. Beyoncé is the performer of our lives. For three hours, she created a space to escape the world and just let loose. Pure transcendence through performance.

Solo Record of The Year

Magic - Kiana Lede

A recording that was not only on replay, replay, replay but also captured the essence of the year. Kiana Lede's "Magic" represents that perfect solo track that defines a moment in time - infectious, memorable, and utterly captivating from first listen.

Album of The Year

Grudges - Kiana Lede

"Grudges" represents the pinnacle of artistic vision - every track flows seamlessly into the next, creating a complete listening experience that rewards both casual listeners and deep cuts enthusiasts.

Artist of The Year

Beyoncé

Honoring the artist who dominated the year with exceptional creativity, impact, and presence. From Renaissance to the world tour that redefined live performance, Beyoncé continues to set the standard for artistry, creative innovation, and cultural influence. The artist of our lives, indeed.
